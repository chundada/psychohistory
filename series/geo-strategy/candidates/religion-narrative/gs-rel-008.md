## 原文引用
Remember Dante in Divine Comedy said that the future is not what happens to you, it is not something that you wait for. The future is what you imagine and fight for. The future is what you make happen. There is a God but God gave us the ability to imagine and the capacity to love.

## 中文解读
以但丁《神曲》的核心理念作为课程总结：未来不是等待你发生的命运，而是你用想象力和爱去创造的东西。这赋予了心理史学一种神学维度的希望——虽然未来可以被预测，但它依然可以被改变。
