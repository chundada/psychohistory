## 原文引用
It seems as though we figure out history. It seems we are able to predict the future by using game theory properly and systematically. But if the future is predictable, does that mean there is no free will? Does that mean God has already written the script? This is the tension between divine providence and human agency.

## 中文解读
当历史规律可以被预测时，就产生了神学难题：如果上帝的剧本已经写好，人类自由意志还有意义吗？这触及了心理史学的根本哲学张力——预定论与自由意志之间的古老争论。
