## 原文引用
John Calhoun constructed this experiment. Ultimately it led to the collapse of the rat utopia. The rats, when they had too many males, would fight each other constantly for status. The females would stop reproducing. The young would be abandoned. And eventually the entire colony died out.

## 中文解读
约翰-卡尔霍恩的"大鼠乌托邦"实验展示了种群密度过高时的崩溃模式。这一实验被用作西方文明现状的生物学隐喻：当社会资源不足以支撑庞大人口时，社会功能开始瓦解。
