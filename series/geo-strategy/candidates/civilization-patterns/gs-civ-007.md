## 原文引用
America thinks it can police the world while its infrastructure crumbles at home. This is imperial hubris. The American Empire is overextended and it cannot sustain itself. The cost of maintaining 800 military bases around the world while Americans cannot afford healthcare is the fundamental contradiction.

## 中文解读
指出帝国过度扩张的悖论：全球军事霸权与国内民生衰败之间的根本矛盾。这是帝国衰落的经典症状——当帝国维持霸权的成本超过其收益时，内部瓦解就开始了。
