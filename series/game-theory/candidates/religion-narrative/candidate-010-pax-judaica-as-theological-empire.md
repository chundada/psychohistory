## Analysis

This candidate reveals **Pax Judaica as a theological-political narrative** that merges biblical prophecy (Greater Israel from the Nile to the Euphrates) with transhumanist technology (AI surveillance state, digital ID as the "mark of the beast"). The lecturer presents this not as a fringe belief but as an active geopolitical project with institutional backing. The narrative structure is tripartite: (1) the biblical promise of land to Abraham, (2) the Kabbalistic concept of Israel as the divine center of the world, and (3) the Freemasonic/technocratic vision of a one-world government controlled by AI. The "mark of the beast" is demythologized as a literal digital ID system. The lecturer claims that Iran is deliberately left out of Greater Israel because Israel's real enemy is the United States, not Iran — the war is designed to force America out of the Middle East.
