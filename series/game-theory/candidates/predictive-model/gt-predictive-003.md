## 原文引用 (English original)
"There are three major reasons that we learned in this class as to why the United States attacked Iran. ... The first major explanation is geopolitics. ... Number two is eschatological. ... The third reason is actually the most mundane: imperial decline."

"What's interesting about these three major reasons is that they're actually not in competition with each other. In fact, they support each other and honestly they lead to the same outcomes. ... If you believe in the eschatological model, you believe that this is the end of days. ... If you just analyze the geopolitical framework, you can actually come to the same conclusions. If you look at the imperial decline framework, you can also come to the same conclusions. This is really strange in that you have these three divergent explanations, but in many ways they're very similar. In fact, they lead to the same conclusions."

"So, my explanation to you is this: Eschatology is really just an understanding of geopolitics framed allegorically."
