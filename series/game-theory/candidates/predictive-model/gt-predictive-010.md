## 原文引用 (English original)
"Historical analogies is a really powerful way to understand the present. So, the Trump visit today is analogous to Nixon's visit in 1972. ... The real reason Nixon visited China is that in 1971, Nixon removed the US dollar from the gold standard. And now the US dollar is worth nothing. What you do now is create a demand for it. So Nixon did two things: first, create the petrodollar. Second, open up China to turn it into a manufacturing base using US dollars."

"Putin's defense pact with North Korea makes a lot of sense. Because if North Korea just creates a problem in the Korean Peninsula, this is going to be a huge problem for Japan and the United States. And remember, the United States is already distracted in Iraq."

"War creates political tensions. The longer the war drags on, the greater the tension is. This is what happened in Russia during World War I when the Bolsheviks came into power. So Putin wants to create a civil war in Europe, allow these right-wing, pro-Russia parties to come into power, and then form an alliance."

## 中文解读
该预测方法的核心是"深层结构类比"：不比较表面的历史事件相似性，而是寻找驱动结构的相似性。1972年尼克松访华是因为美元脱离金本位后需要制造新的美元需求，因此将中国纳入美元体系。2026年特朗普访华同样因为美元体系濒临崩溃，需要将中国储蓄转化为美债购买力。同时，普京援引一战俄国革命的教训，预测通过长期战争在欧洲引发政治动荡并催生亲俄右翼政府上台。
