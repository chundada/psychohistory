## 原文引用 (English original)
"So, today I want to introduce you to a new theory of game theory called the law of eschatological convergence. ... Eschatology, what it is is a story of how the world ends. ... Different traditions will have different eschatologies. And what's important to figure out is in what aspect do these eschatologies converge. Converge, meaning a union of these eschatologies. And why is that important? Because it allows you to predict how they will behave. Think of a story as the operating system of a society. And as such, it's a script that they will act out. And if you're able to figure out the different scripts and see how they converge together, where the union of these different scripts are, then you can actually predict how the world will turn out."

"And again, I keep on saying this, but purely from a geopolitical perspective, purely from a realpolitik perspective, this war in Iran makes no sense. But, if you look at it from an eschatological perspective, then it makes perfect sense."

"So, what I'm trying to show you is that just by analyzing the most extreme eschatologies of different religious traditions, you can then from this analysis pick out points in which they agree. And at these points, you can figure out then how they will affect current geopolitics to make certain predictions about how the war will progress, about how the world will transform itself next 2 to 4 years."

## 中文解读
该框架的核心方法论是：不从地缘政治或经济利益出发分析国际冲突，而是研究各主要文明中最极端的末世论版本，找到它们的汇聚点（convergence points）。一旦找到这些汇聚点，就能预测各行为体的行为——因为末世论本质上是社会的"操作系统"，各派会按照剧本行动。讲座展示了六大末世论传统（琐罗亚斯德教、犹太教、基督教锡安主义、伊斯兰教、天主教、东正教）如何在"第三圣殿建立""敌基督崛起""Gog-Magog之战"等关键节点上汇聚。
