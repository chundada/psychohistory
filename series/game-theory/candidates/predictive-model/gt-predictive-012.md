## 原文引用 (English original)
"Iran is defending itself using something called the Mosaic defense, which is to say that there are 31 provinces and these are 31 cells or units that are all responsible for their own strategy and attack and defense. Meaning that there's no longer a centralized command. So it doesn't matter if you negotiate with the leadership of Iran because the IRGC, the people who are fighting, will not agree to a ceasefire because they do not trust the Americans or the Israelis."

"Think of the Mosaic defense as almost a dead man switch where if you kill the Ayatollah, the Ayatollah will tell his people to fight to the end. The Mosaic defense will only come to an end if either Iran is defeated or the Americans are defeated. This thing collapses if there's a ceasefire because in order for the Iranians to implement a ceasefire, they themselves will have to go and control these Mosaic units in the provinces, which basically means a civil war."

## 中文解读
该框架通过分析对手的军事/组织结构来预测冲突走向。伊朗的"马赛克防御"（31个自治单元+去中心化指挥）是一个天然的"死机开关"——任何外部谈判达成的停火都无法执行，因为基层单元不服从中央命令。这意味着：一旦战争开始，它要么打到最后一方彻底失败，要么伊朗先打一场内战才能停火。该框架可用于预测任何去中心化组织（如恐怖网络、分布式叛乱）的冲突是否真正可谈判。
