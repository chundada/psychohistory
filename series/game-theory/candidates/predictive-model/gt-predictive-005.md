## 原文引用 (English original)
"The third reason is actually the most mundane: imperial decline. When an empire declines, it does all sorts of stupid things. Because internally it is fracturing for three reasons. The first reason is financialization: a few people control all the capital, force everyone into debt slavery. ... Second problem is demographic crisis: young people refuse to have children, old people live longer. ... The third reason is something called elite overproduction, a phrase coined by historian Peter Turchin. He argues that if you look at all societies in decline, it's driven by the fact that you have certain elites who want to control policy who want power, but power by definition is a zero-sum game. So these elites compete for limited positions of power, and this leads to civil war. One manifestation of the civil war is that this war is projected outwards."

## 中文解读
该框架提供了帝国衰退的可预测指标（leading indicators）：(1) 金融化——资本集中+债务奴役；(2) 人口危机——年轻人不生育+老龄化；(3) 精英过度生产（Peter Turchin的理论）——过多精英争夺有限权力位置导致内战。当这三个指标同时出现时，可预测帝国将向外投射冲突（如无缘无故入侵他国）来转移内部矛盾。这解释了美国同时攻击伊朗、谈论吞并加拿大/格陵兰等看似非理性的行为。
