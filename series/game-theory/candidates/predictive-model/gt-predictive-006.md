## 原文引用 (English original)
"For each country I want you to imagine each country as a chess set. So this is a chess set and I want to show you how each player behaves using this chess set as a template. So the king is the political system. The first idea to understand is that when you fight a war, you're not trying to defeat the other person's military. What you're really trying to do is you're trying to put your opponent in check by attacking the political system. The queen is the grand strategy — how each player perceives geopolitics and how to best dominate geopolitics. And then you have the bishop, the knight and the rook — the attack vectors, your mechanisms for achieving your grand strategy. And now you have the pawn — sacrificial tools that you can use in order to advance your grand strategy."

"The grand strategy is not something that you just make up. Grand strategy is an expression, a manifestation of your political system and your cultural system."

## 中文解读
该框架提供了一个系统的预测模板：将每个国家解构为国际象棋棋局——王=政治系统（容易被攻击的弱点）、后=大战略（文化世界观的外化）、象/马/车=攻击手段、兵=消耗品。每个国家都有其独特的"将死"方式（如美国怕内战、俄罗斯怕继承危机、伊朗怕极端化分裂、以色列怕内部分裂）。通过分析各方的大战略和文化基础文本（如美国的Paradise Lost vs 俄罗斯的Crime and Punishment），可以预测其在冲突中的行为模式。
