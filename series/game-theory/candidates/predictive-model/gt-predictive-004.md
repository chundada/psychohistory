## 原文引用 (English original)
"First thing you need to understand is that history moves in a certain direction. There is a structure and a pattern to the movement of history. Think of a river. ... There are certain individuals we can call them occultists who try to read the river or try to read historical patterns in history and they create something called eschatology. ... Then from these occultists form secret societies in order to understand this eschatology and also to promote it. Now what happens is that powerful people, the rich and powerful, they join these societies. ... But the problem is that you need people to actually move towards the ending to propel this eschatology. And these people we can call agents. ... And this is how the world works, where the occultists are the ones who imagine the world, and the rich and powerful are the ones who act out the world, and then they appoint certain agents who are like puppets in order to move the world in a certain direction."

"Secret societies do not believe they can change the world. They cannot alter the course of history. What they believe is they can ride the wave of history into absolute power."

## 中文解读
该框架将历史动力归结为三层结构：底层是"神秘主义者"（occultists），他们感知历史模式并创造末世叙事；中层是"秘密社团"（secret societies），由追求权力秩序控制的精英组成；表层是"代理人"（agents），如特朗普、普京等被推到前台的历史人物，他们自认为在完成神圣使命。预测的关键在于理解底层叙事脚本——因为它决定了整个系统的运动方向。
