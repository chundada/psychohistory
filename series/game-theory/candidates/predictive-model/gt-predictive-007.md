## 原文引用 (English original)
"Reality is a hallucination. It is something that we collectively imagine together. And again, the metaphor I use is Plato's allegory of the cave, where you imagine about a million people chained together, and they're all looking at a wall. Behind the people, is this great fire, where the elite project shadows onto the wall. The people who are chained don't know what's going on behind the scenes. They only see what's in front of them, which is the wall. They see the shadows, and they start to create a reality around the shadows. ... The main message here is that true wealth is our attention. Our consciousness is what's real. Everything else is an illusion. What power is, is the capacity to direct and control this attention."

"From this hallucination, I can actually project myself onto another force and create another hallucination. That's what Richard Nixon did. He created two new hallucinations — GCC and China."
