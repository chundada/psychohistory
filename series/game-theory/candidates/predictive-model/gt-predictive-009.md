## 原文引用 (English original)
"The cabala shows you where the universe came from and where it's going. The main logic of the universe is the movement of thesis, antithesis and synthesis. ... What the people in the cabala believe is that there is an inevitable process to the universe and you can't stop it. What you can do is you can accelerate the process. So if the movement is thesis, antithesis, synthesis, one way to understand this is for us to be good, we must first be evil. Because what evil does is it annoys God and God punishes you. Then you seek forgiveness from God and then God forgives you and then you become better. But you must first commit sin in order to redeem yourself. You can't stop this process. What you can do is you can accelerate this process."

"The idea of sin: you must break yourself before you can learn the wisdom to welcome God into your heart. ... The story of King David — he commits adultery and murder, God punishes him, he breaks his heart, and then he welcomes God back. Nothing matters except your relationship with God. The whole world could be in nuclear war. Doesn't matter. What matters is your true faith in God."

## 中文解读
该框架揭示了基于喀巴拉神秘主义的"通过作恶加速救赎"预测逻辑：宇宙遵循正-反-合的辩证运动，不可停止但可以加速。因此以色列的行为逻辑是——主动制造冲突和"邪恶"（如摧毁阿克萨清真寺、压迫巴勒斯坦），因为这能"激怒上帝"，迫使上帝惩罚以色列，然后以色列在忏悔中与上帝重归于好，从而加速救赎进程。这解释了为何以色列不寻求和平，反而主动升级冲突——他们不是在追求地缘政治利益，而是在执行一个加速末日-救赎的神学剧本。
