## 原文引用 (English original)
"I make these predictions in order to validate my prediction model, but just because these predictions turn out to be correct does not validate my prediction model 100%. ... It's important to keep an open mind and to continue to make predictions."

"When I first started making predictions two years ago, I was just looking at patterns and then trying to figure out what patterns were the most salient or the most obvious. And I made those predictions and I was fairly confident about them. Now that I'm famous, now that people are demanding I make predictions every day, it's hard for me to make accurate predictions because I don't have access to all the information and it's hard for me to think clearly objectively because I know that whatever I say will have an impact on global events. I am no longer an observer, I'm a player."

"It's possible that we'll never really understand causation. ... I myself appreciate the limitations to my own objectivity because of my status today."

## 中文解读
该框架是关于预测方法论本身的元认知框架：(1) 预测正确并不等于模型正确——相关性≠因果性；(2) 当预测者本身被纳入系统后（因出名而成为博弈参与者），其预测精度会下降（观察者效应）；(3) 需要持续用新预测检验和修正模型，而不可因一次成而固化和教条化。讲座中的教授坦诚自己成名后面临的信息污染和立场偏差，这是所有预测者都需要警惕的认知陷阱。

## 可应用场景
任何预测者在做出预测后，必须持续追踪真实结果与预测的偏差，并将偏差反馈到模型修正中。同时警惕"预测者效应"——当你因预测准确而出名后，你的预测本身会影响被预测的系统的走向。
