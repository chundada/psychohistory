## 原文引用 (English original)
"What America is doing is, when they mean 'prioritize strategic interest,' what they mean is exerting control over the world's choke points so that they can control global trade. They're not going to control the entire ocean because it's much too big. But if they control certain choke points like the Middle East and Malacca, that's probably enough."

"The point is to get China to submit. The point is to control China, not to destroy China. ... America has a blue water navy that controls the oceans. ... The key choke points: Strait of Hormuz, Strait of Malacca, Strait of Gibraltar, Panama Canal, Cape of Good Hope. If you control them, you control who gets what resources."

"What America can do that other nations cannot is basically retreat into its continental fortress and control sea trade. America controls the seas with its navies. So what America can do is allow its ships to go anywhere in the world and block Chinese, Iranian, Russian ships from accessing the rest of the world."

## 中文解读
该框架通过全球咽喉点的控制权分布来预测大国博弈格局。核心洞察：控制全部海洋是不现实的，但控制大约6-8个关键咽喉点（霍尔木兹、马六甲、直布罗陀、巴拿马、好望角等）就能控制全球贸易的绝大部分。美国的大战略是：确保这些咽喉点由美国或其盟友控制 → 通过选择性开放/封闭来控制特定国家的资源获取 → 迫使挑战者接受美国的交易条件（如用美元结算、购买美债）。该预测框架适用于分析中美俄三方的长期博弈。
