## 原文引用 (English original)
"The universal law of game theory states this. Whoever wins a game is able to do this: Mass times energy times coordination. ... Mass is just the number of people involved on the team. Energy is just how much attention, focus, motivation there is. How invested are you in winning the game? And coordination, it's just the idea of synchronicity. How well do the teammates work together? ... The most important thing in winning a game is actually coordination. In fact, I would say it's four times more important than just mass. ... Energy is twice as important as mass."

"The most powerful mechanism in history is eschatology. ... In narrative, the most powerful form of narrative is eschatology. Because eschatology is a complete script in which everyone knows what he or she must do in order to achieve the desired outcome. And eschatology also is able to transcend both time and space."

## 中文解读
这是一个预测博弈胜负的量化框架：胜负 = 质量 × 能量 × 协调。其中协调的重要性是质量的4倍，能量是质量的2倍。而增强协调的最佳长期机制是"叙事"（narrative），叙事中最强的是"末世论"（eschatology），因为它提供完整的剧本，让参与者跨越时空自动协调。这一框架解释了为何少数极端分子（高能量+高协调）能战胜数量远大于他们的主流群体。
