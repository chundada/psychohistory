GT01-The-Dating-Game

## 原文引用 (English)
"According to superstructure, the game is going to change. So, let's go back to the game, okay? We have 10 people. In a low population poor society, guess what? They're not going to date. What they're going to do is they might get married, but they're going to have sex with each other. Why? Because as a woman, the only way to ensure that your child survives is if all the men in the village want to protect and nurture the child... Now, in a superstructure number two, there's no dating either. Why? Because this society is in competition with other societies, and they need to have as many children as possible. So, what the the solution here is to have arranged marriages... Superstructure number three is the one we live in. And here you have a dating game. Right? Because why? Because the odds of you obtaining status is really, really low, okay? And the only way that you can change your status is by marrying up."

## 中文解读
文明的演化受其"超级结构"（demographics, economics, culture, politics, religion）驱动，分为三个阶段：第一阶段（低人口/贫困社会）以乱交维持社会凝聚力；第二阶段（增长人口/竞争社会）以包办婚姻最大化生育以应对外部威胁；第三阶段（人口过剩/富裕社会）以"约会游戏"为主导，人们为地位而非繁衍结合。每个阶段决定了不同的婚姻制度、性别关系和生育策略，而第三阶段的地位零和博弈必然导致生育率崩溃和文明衰落。
