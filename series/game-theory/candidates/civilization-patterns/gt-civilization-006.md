## 原文引用 (English)
"So, the Romans started out as mercenaries for the Etruscans in Italy. The Aztecs started out as mercenaries for the Colhua Mexica in Mexico. The Greeks started out as mercenaries for the Persians, okay? The Mongols started out as mercenaries for the Chinese. The Qin started out as mercenaries for the Sumerians. The Mamluks Mamluks started out as mercenaries for the Ayyubid dynasty in Egypt. In every instance, what happened is the mercenaries eventually got to the point where, 'We are the ones killing ourselves for this empire. The empire is corrupt, decadent, and weak. Why don't we just take it over?' And they did, okay? We're seeing a similar situation play out today in the Middle East where Israel was created by the British and Americans to be the to to be their pitbull..."

## 中文解读
这是贯穿人类文明的帝国替代模式：一个帝国招募边缘群体作为雇佣兵（或代理人）来执行暴力任务，但雇佣兵在过程中变得更有凝聚力、更有战斗意志，而帝国则日益腐败、堕落、软弱。最终雇佣兵意识到"我们才是真正卖命的人，帝国已腐朽，为什么我们不取而代之？"罗马取代伊特鲁里亚、阿兹特克取代科尔瓦、蒙古取代中国王朝、马穆鲁克取代阿尤布王朝，都是这一模式的体现。GT16指出，今天的以色列正扮演着美国帝国的"雇佣兵"角色，正经历从代理到替代的转变。
