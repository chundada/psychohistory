## 原文引用 (English)
"So, he proposed the idea of aasayiah. And all this word means is basically cohesion or group solidarity. His theory is that yes, the people in the margins, they're poorer, but because they're poorer, they're more unified. They are more they focus more on solidarity, on working together. Whereas in the rich areas, they become too individualistic. They become too decent, too corrupt. And ultimately that's why it's always these tribes from the margins that actually [snorts] conquer the wealthy civilized areas. Okay? And this is a pattern that repeat repeats itself throughout human history all the time. So it's almost like an iron law of history."

"And if you look at most of human history, this pattern repeats itself. If you look at a region, the strongest nation does not come out on top. It's usually the weakest most marginalized area that will eventually come and conquer the entire territory and create an empire."

## 中文解读
这是文明兴衰的核心定律——阿萨比亚（群体凝聚力/团结精神）。伊本·赫勒敦提出的这一理论指出，富裕的中心文明会因财富而变得个人主义、腐败和自满，而边缘地带的贫困群体因生存压力而保持团结、纪律和牺牲精神，最终征服中心。从秦朝统一中国、马其顿征服希腊城邦、罗马崛起、阿兹特克建国到西班牙征服阿兹特克，这一模式在整个人类历史中反复重演，几乎成为一条"铁律"。
