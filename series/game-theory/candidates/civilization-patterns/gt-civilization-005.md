## 原文引用 (English)
"During the war states period, it was a game to see which state would be the best, the most innovative, and that's why you invite people who um could contribute the most to your city state. Okay, that's why you were meritocracy. You only pro promoted the best and the brightest. Once you reach an equilibrium, you don't have to do that. Okay, all you have to do is just maintain the status quo. And so what happens is you create this rigid hierarchy."

"In the first game, the religion is about energy. It's about creativity. It's about working together. Okay. But then in the second iteration, the religion becomes like a bureaucracy which is like you must follow the rules of the game. Okay. The rules of the game are set. There's a hierarchy and you must obey this hierarchy."

## 中文解读
文明从充满活力的"战国阶段"走向"均衡帝国"的过程，本质上是制度硬化的过程。战国时期城市国家间开放竞争，推崇贤能政治，奖励创新和人才。但一旦某个势力统一（达成均衡），竞争消失，体系从贤能政治退化为刚性等级制度。原有的富有创造力的宗教（或意识形态）也退化为官僚化的规则体系。此时宫廷政治和派系斗争取代了开放竞争，文明失去适应能力，最终被边缘群体征服。
