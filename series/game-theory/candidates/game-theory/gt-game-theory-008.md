## 原文引用 (English)
"Whenever you play a game, you're not just playing one game. You're playing many, many different games. And the game that you choose to play is the one that is most proximate to you, the one that is close most close to you. And that impacts your decision-making the most. Okay? So that's the law of proximity."

"As you can see, every single day you're playing multiple games even though even though you may not recognize it. And again, what's important is the game that is most important to you is the game which you are most close to, right? The one that you can see in front of you."

"People are much more interested in their own internal conflict than the outside global conflict."

## 中文解读
邻近法则是该系列对"多博弈重叠"问题的核心解决方案。讲者指出，任何个体/国家在任何时刻都参与多个嵌套博弈（家庭、学校、工作、城市、国家等），而最影响决策的永远是那个"最靠近你"的博弈。这解释了为什么在美伊战争中，美国、以色列、伊朗的内部冲突（党派斗争、宗教分裂）比外部战争更决定国家行为——内部博弈比外部博弈更"邻近"玩家。
