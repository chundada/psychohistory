## 原文引用 (English)
"A game is constructed when all the different players agree on the rules and incentives of the game. All right, I was an outsider. I was not a player. I came in to construct a game in which I felt that students would thrive and they did thrive. But that's not what they want. Okay? They wanted to have control over the game."

"So, think of you have different sets, right? Okay. Parents, teachers, students, government, colleges. Okay. And their convergence point is the game they play. Okay. Now, what's important for us to understand is that there is actually a lot of room to this convergence point. So it's possible to do reform but only within a subset of of this convergence point."

## 中文解读
这个框架解释了社会系统的"粘性"——为什么明知现状糟糕却难以改变。讲者以学校教育为例，识别出学生、家长、教师、管理者、政府和大学等多个利益相关者。每个群体都有自己的真实动机（而非宣称的动机），这些动机的"收敛点"（convergence point）就是实际运行的博弈。改革者只能在这个收敛点允许的范围内调整，否则会被系统排斥。
