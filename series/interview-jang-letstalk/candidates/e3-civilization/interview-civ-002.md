## 原文引用
And what this what this tells me and this is really really important to appreciate
is the military bureaucracy has become dislodged from reality itself. They don't
care if they win the war or not, right? Because they don't suffer any
consequences. If you are the Iranians and you were to lose this war, well, the
entire elite would be replaced. ... But the worst that can happen maybe 10 years
from now when this war is totally lost, Congress will call these generals before
Congress in a congressional testimony and they'll shout at these generals for
two hours and that'll be it, right? That's the extent of the consequences for
losing a war for America.

## 中文解读
这是Jang的核心文明论观察之一：美国军事官僚体系已经"脱离现实本身"（dislodged from reality）。由于失败没有真正的后果——国会质询两小时即是最坏结果——体系内缺乏纠错机制。相比之下，伊朗精英一旦战败将面临整个阶层的消灭，这种生存压力决定了两个体系在战争中的投入差异。
