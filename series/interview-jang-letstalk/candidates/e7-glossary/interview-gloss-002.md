## 原文引用
What the yen carry trade is this because of deflation in the Japanese economy the
bank of Japan has been trying to print as much money as possible to create
liquidity in the economy. So what they've been doing is they've been lending
money to Japanese corporations and banks at zero interest. ... But because these
are companies and they're only interested in profit, what they do is they take
this money and then buy US treasuries which pays 5% interest, right? And this
this this is what's called the the yan carry trade.

## 中文解读
Jang详细定义了"日元套利交易"（yen carry trade）这一关键全球经济机制：日本央行以零利率向国内企业放贷→企业用这些资金购买收益率为5%的美国国债→实质上是日本在补贴美国经济。这一机制是全球流动性体系的核心支柱，而其取消将导致美国经济泡沫破裂。
