## 原文引用
Which Americans suffered because of the loss of uh Vietnam? I mean because
of Afghanistan, because of Iraq. Americans have been losing wars for the
past few decades. And there have been actually no consequences. But the
military-industrial complex has gotten wealthier and wealthier over these past
few decades. Go to Washington DC. Go to Fairfax County. Go to Alexandria.
They're filthy rich. Right. So, so it's a perverse situation where America is

clear path to victory.

## 中文解读
这是一个经典的"逆向激励"（perverse incentive）博弈分析：美国的军政体制中，失败没有成本（no consequences），而军工复合体因战争持续而不断获利。这种激励结构导致美国陷入"为战而战"的锁定状态——即使明知无法取胜，体系内部缺乏终止博弈的机制。
