## 原文引用
Which Americans suffered because of the loss of uh Vietnam? I mean because of
Afghanistan, because of Iraq. Americans have been losing wars for the past
few decades. And there have been actually no consequences. But the
military-industrial complex has gotten wealthier and wealthier over these past
few decades. ... The worst that can happen maybe 10 years from now when this war
is totally lost, Congress will call these generals before Congress in a
congressional testimony and they'll shout at these generals for two hours and
that'll be it, right? That's the extent of the consequences for losing a war
for America.

## 中文解读
Jang揭示了美国战争机器中的一个根本性制度缺陷：战败没有真实后果。从越南到阿富汗到伊拉克，几十年的战争失败没有带来问责——国会质询两小时就是最坏结果。在缺乏"失败成本"（failure cost）的情况下，决策者没有任何动力去避免战争或反思错误，这是激励结构的系统性失灵。
