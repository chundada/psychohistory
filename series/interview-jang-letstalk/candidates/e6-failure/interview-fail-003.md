## 原文引用
And what this what this tells me and this is really really important to appreciate
is the military bureaucracy has become dislodged from reality itself. They don't
care if they win the war or not, right? Because they don't suffer any
consequences. ... we know you send in these massive warships against an entrenched
enemy with drones. There's nothing you can do. You can't actually hurt them, but

though you are not even though you know it's not effectual.

## 中文解读
Jang提出一个深刻的制度性失败模式：美国军事官僚体系已经"脱离现实本身"（dislodged from reality itself）。即使高层明知当前战略无效（not effectual），仍然持续执行，因为失败没有个人成本。这是制度性认知失调（institutional cognitive dissonance）的典型案例——组织失去了与现实对接的反馈回路。
